Project: Algorithmic Trading Dashboard (PaperTrade)
Tech stack: Python, Node.js, Alpaca API, AWS EC2, AWS S3.
Live URL: https://api.gary-yong.com/trading

Built a full-stack algorithmic trading platform with a Python quantitative engine connected to the Alpaca paper trading API. The platform supports both manual trading and fully automated strategy execution.

Implemented 5 algorithmic trading strategies:
- Momentum/Trend Following: RSI, MACD, EMA crossover signals
- Mean Reversion: Bollinger Bands, Z-score deviation
- Volatility Breakout: ATR-based stop-loss and max-hold exits
- Value & Dividends: RSI-based confidence scoring
- Sentiment Analysis: news-driven signal generation

Phase 2 additions include a Backtesting Panel with equity curve vs SPY benchmark, underwater drawdown chart, monthly returns heatmap, and key metrics (Sharpe, Sortino, Calmar, win rate, profit factor, VaR). Also shipped a Risk Dashboard with drawdown curves, Sharpe ratio cards per strategy, position concentration heatmap, and daily loss limit progress bars.

Risk controls: maximum 20% per position, 80% max invested, stop losses, daily loss limits.

All dashboard pages are publicly accessible — no login required.

Tags: algorithmic trading, Python, Alpaca API, quant finance, backtesting, risk management, Node.js, AWS, Sharpe ratio, momentum trading.
